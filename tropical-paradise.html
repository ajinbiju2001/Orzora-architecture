THE VISION

A Sanctuary of
Passive Equilibrium.

Tropical Paradise represents a meticulous dialogue between traditional vernacular wisdom and contemporary structural rigour. Situated in the humid landscapes of Kerala, the project explores the "void" as a functional cooling mechanism.

By integrating expansive overhangs and a multi-layered skin of bamboo screens and stone gabions, we achieved a thermal performance that eliminates the need for active climate control, allowing the inhabitant to exist in constant synchronicity with the landscape.

Structural Logic

Concept & Diagrammatic Exploration

01. Thermal Chimneys

Strategically placed vertical voids allow hot air to rise and escape, drawing cool air across the internal water bodies at ground level.

02. Floating Planes

Horizontal slabs are separated from vertical walls by continuous glass strips, creating the illusion of gravity-defying shelter.

03. Living Filter

Dense tropical vegetation acts as a biological air filter and visual screen, blurring the boundaries between interior and exterior.
